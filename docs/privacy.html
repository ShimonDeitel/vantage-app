Privacy Policy

Last updated July 17, 2026

Vantage is a sketch-proportion coaching app. This policy explains, plainly, what the app does with your camera, your photos, and any data it touches.

Camera & the live overlay

The free live overlay (proportion grid and plumb line) reads the camera feed and the device's motion sensors entirely on your device, in real time, to draw the grid and crosshair. Nothing from the live overlay is recorded, saved, or sent anywhere.

Photos used for AI critique (Pro)

When you use the Pro critique feature, the reference photo and your sketch photo are sent to a stateless AI inference relay operated by the developer (a Cloudflare Worker in front of Cloudflare Workers AI) solely to generate the proportion comparison. That relay does not require an account, does not attach the images to any identity, and does not retain the images after the response is returned. Vantage does not control what any upstream AI infrastructure provider logs at the transport level for abuse prevention, but no persistent copy of your photos is kept by Vantage's own backend.

Session history

If you save a critique, the reference photo, your sketch photo, and the feedback are stored locally on your device only, using Apple's on-device SwiftData storage. This history is never uploaded anywhere. Deleting the app, or using "Erase All Data" in Settings, permanently removes it.

What Vantage does not do

  • No account or sign-in of any kind.
  • No advertising and no ad SDKs.
  • No third-party analytics or behavioral tracking.
  • No sale or sharing of your photos or data with any third party for their own purposes.

Subscriptions & payment

Vantage Pro is an auto-renewable subscription billed through your Apple ID. Payment, billing, and cancellation are handled entirely by Apple under its own App Store terms and privacy practices — Vantage never receives your payment details.

Children

Vantage is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change and the new policy will be posted at this same address.
